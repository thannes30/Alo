FAQ

What services does Alo Cinema One, Inc. provide?

We specialize in installing and dismantling all types of material handling equipment. Including but not limited to: pallet rack, pallet flow, cantilever rack, shelving, mezzanines, lockers, drive In rack, push back rack and especially AS/RS systems.

What are your areas of operation?

We work in the entire continental United States, also Canada, Mexico and Caribbean Islands.

What is the minimum size project Alo Cinema will work?

We will work in any material handling project, no matter how big or small.

How many workers are dedicated to a project?

We are aware that each customer’s installation needs are specific, so we take the time to get to know you and tailor our efforts so that we can fulfill your exact requirements and guidelines. On every project we have a dedicated project manager, lead foreman, junior foreman and laboring crew. The size of the project and most importantly, the schedule, will determine the number of workers necessary to staff the project accordingly and meet any deadlines.

What kind of experience do Alo Cinema employees bring?

Our team is comprised of over 150 full time highly skilled laborers and trained equipment operators. We have 15 foreman/supervisors with combined experience of over 200 years.

How is Alo Cinema equipped to perform work?

All of our crews are equipped with company owned vehicles as well as tools, which will guarantee that each job will be completed in a timely and cost effective manner. We also work hand in hand with national equipment and supply vendors.

Does Alo Cinema buy or sell material handling equipment?

No. Unlike other installation companies, we do not buy or sell material handling equipment, but focus entirely on installing and dismantling.

Is Alo Cinema Insured?

Yes. Our company is fully insured with a $5,000,000.00 umbrella policy and bondable as well.

Does Alo Cinema work with prevailing wages?

Yes. While most of our jobs are done with non- prevailing wages, we also offer the ability to provide prevailing wage, as well as, union labor on jobs where it’s required
